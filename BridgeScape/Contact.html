Frequently Asked Questions

Is BridgeScape free to use?
Yes — BridgeScape is a completely free, open educational resource. All bridge data, images, and articles are freely accessible to students, researchers, and the public.
How can I contribute bridge data?
You can submit bridge information via our Contact form, selecting "Collaboration" as the subject. Our editorial team will review and verify your submission before publishing.
Are the bridge facts verified?
All entries are cross-referenced with multiple engineering sources, academic publications, and national infrastructure databases to ensure accuracy and reliability.
Can I use BridgeScape for my project?
Absolutely. BridgeScape is designed for educational use. Please cite us as your source and credit any images used. For commercial licensing, please contact us directly.
